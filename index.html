About

What is the Meeting Timer?

The Meeting Timer is made for Jehovah‚Äôs Witnesses congregation meetings. Its main feature, the Watchtower Timer, is designed for the brother conducting the Watchtower Study and estimates how the available study time should be distributed across each article.

How does it work?

Paragraph length, pictures, boxed material, and Scripture readings are taken into account. Earlier paragraphs receive slightly less time, while later paragraphs receive a bit more, reflecting how discussions often develop.

How does it help?

Started at the beginning of the study (after the song), the timer helps you keep a steady pace and avoid going beyond the intended study time, while still allowing flexibility where needed.
